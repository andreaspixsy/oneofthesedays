One of These Days, this blog was going to change

Puns aside, I’ve now completely switched blogging platforms, migrating off of Wordpress to a custom Jekyll setup. While wordpress was a seriously powerful platform, I was only using ~2% of its functionality, and that 2% wasn’t doing quite what I wanted. Now, with Jekyll, running on Sinatra on Heroku, I can hack things to work as needed. For example, posts can be pre-processed with Docco which creates the amazing styling on this page.
