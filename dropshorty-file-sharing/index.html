Here’s a quick demo of an awesome OSX app, DropShorty

Once you’ve downloaded it, you need to enter you Dropbox user ID. You can find this on your Dropbox folder online by going in to your shared folder, right clicking on a file and selecting “Copy public link”. You then need to copy the number in the url, as shown below:

Once you’ve set this (in Preferences ? + ,) , you can then connect it to to one of your URL shortening accounts.
